History

In 1930, Snickers first appeared. After the family's beloved horse at the time, the candies was given that name. A common mix of ingredients included nougat, peanuts, and caramel. In the UK and Ireland, the sweets bar was offered for sale under the name Marathon bar until 1990.

Snickers was eventually offered in various forms due to its rising popularity. The line-up of Snickers products expanded to include protein bars, mini Snickers, a dark chocolate variety, ice cream products, and more. King-size sweets bars were no longer available in the UK after the Manifesto for Food and Health of 2004 mandated that they be downsized.
